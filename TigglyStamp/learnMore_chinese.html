What are Tiggly Shapes?

Learn with Tiggly! Help your preschooler develop spatial reasoning and motor skills by playing with Tiggly Shapes.

Tiggly Shapes are rubberized toys that children between 18 months and 4 years old can use for learning and play with an iPad. The shapes have a soft plastic cover and silicone touch points that are recognized by our iPad apps.

They are durable enough for toddler play-yet gentle enough for iPad screens.
