How to play:

Play with Tiggly shapes:

  • Simply tap a shape on the screen and watch as the image transforms!
  • Take the shape off the screen to see what happens next.
  • You can drag objects around the screen to design your scene.
  • You may delete an object by dragging it into the garbage can on the right bottom corner.
  • You may start with a new page double tapping the right top corner.
  • When you’re done with your creation, tap the star icon in the top left corner, and two icons appear: camera and video recorder.
  • You can take a picture of the scene by tapping the camera icon.
  • You can record your story in Movie Mode by tapping the video recorder icon. While recording video, you may drag objects around the scene and tell stories about them. The video will save your voice as well as the screen.
