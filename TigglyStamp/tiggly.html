About Tiggly Stamp:

Tiggly Stamp is all about creativity — creating amusing scenes and coming up with inspiring stories. In Tiggly Stamp, children use their shapes to construct a series of fun and imaginative characters, objects, and fruits and veggies related to each of the four seasons. They may also put together pieces to build increasingly complex creations as their development allows. Imagine a fox wearing a sweater holding on a cup of hot chocolate in one hand and a snow shovel in another hand, or a scarecrow raking colorful leaves into a pile…. Your child’s awakening imagination is the limit.

Children can take a picture of the scene they’ve created by tapping on the camera icon, or they can tell a story and create a video by tapping on the video recorder icon.

Tiggly Stamp helps even very young children express themselves, reflect on their own view of the world, and develop their creativity, social, and linguistic skills through storytelling. And keep in mind that stories are shared cultural practices, so get involved and be creative along with them! Let your child’s imagination be the guide and you can follow their lead.
